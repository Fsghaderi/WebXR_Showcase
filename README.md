## ğŸ“± How to Use

1. Open the [demo link](https://fsghaderi.github.io/WebXR_Showcase/) on your **mobile device** (iOS Safari or Android Chrome)
2. Tap the **"Enter AR"** button at the bottom of the screen
3. Allow camera permissions when prompted
4. Browse playground equipment in the bottom carousel
5. Tap an item to select it (white border indicates selection)
6. Move your phone to see a white ring reticle on the ground
7. Tap the screen where the reticle appears to place equipment
8. Continue selecting and placing different components
9. Walk around to view equipment from all angles
10. Tap **"Stop AR"** to exit the experience
